# Joint Journey — Financial Aspiration Plan

_Living document. Last updated: 25 June 2026_

## 🎯 Headline Goal
Build Joint Journey into a **£500k+/year revenue platform worth millions**,
by combining (1) a direct-to-patient prehab + recovery COMMUNITY,
(2) NHS institutional contracts, and (3) expansion to prehab for ANY
major surgery.

> The app alone caps out ~£100k. The COMMUNITY + PLATFORM is the
> £500k-and-millions story.

---

## 🧬 The Three Multipliers (why this can be big)
1. **Community & live engagement** — cook-along nights, live classes,
   peer cohorts, livestreaming. Turns a 6-month tool into a year+
   subscription with high retention and viral word-of-mouth.
2. **Surgical breadth** — prehab is a *surgery* concept, not an ortho one.
   Hip/knee is the wedge; cancer, hernia, gynae, cardiac, abdominal are
   the platform. TAM goes from ~200k/yr to millions of operations/yr.
3. **Outcomes evidence** — your Oxford + length-of-stay data is the moat
   that unlocks NHS contracts and a high valuation multiple.

---

## 📈 Phased Strategy

### Phase 1 — Free Beta via Surgeon Referral (0–6 mo)
- Free to all. Surgeons recommend it.
- Collect usage, testimonials, baseline Oxford Scores.
- Revenue: £0 (investment phase)

### Phase 2 — Community + Early Revenue (6–18 mo)
- Launch community + first live events
- Publish/present early outcomes
- Introduce patient subscription (whole product, no freemium split)
- First NHS pilot conversation
- Revenue: first £20k–80k

### Phase 3 — Scale to £500k+ (18 mo+)
- Patient subscriptions at volume + NHS contracts
- Expand into 1–2 new surgical pathways
- Revenue target: £500k+; position for investment/valuation in the millions

## 💰 Revenue Models
| Model | Price point | Role |
|-------|------------|------|
| **Patient subscription** | £7–12/mo (pre + post-op) | Core growth + community engine |
| **NHS commissioning** | Per-patient tariff / ICB contract | Big-ticket B2B (long waits = best fit) |
| **Live events / premium community** | Incl. in sub, or ticketed specials | Retention + differentiation |
| **Private hospitals** | Per-patient | Minor channel (short waits = less value) |
| **Sponsorship/partnerships** | Variable, ethical only | Supplementary |

## 📊 Market Sizing (UK)
- Ortho wedge: ~200,000 hip+knee/yr, ~160,000+ waiting at any time
- All major surgery (platform vision): millions of operations/yr
- NHS waits are the longest in the developed world = max prehab value

## 📌 KPIs
- Registered / active users; onboarding completion

- Community engagement (event attendance, retention)
- Oxford baseline → follow-up improvement
- Length-of-stay reduction (the NHS selling number)
- Subscription conversion + churn; LTV vs CAC

## ⚠️ Honest Risks
- Older demographic = some friction in paying / using tech
- Engagement window short UNLESS community keeps them post-op
- NHS sales cycles are slow (but high value & high credibility fit)
- Clinical governance/liability must stay tight as you expand pathways
- Big valuation needs real evidence + real retention, not just signups
