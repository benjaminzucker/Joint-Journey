💡 Exercise Tips

  • Warm up first - Walk around the room for 2-3 minutes
  • Hold on to something sturdy - Kitchen worktop or heavy chair
  • Pain is normal, sharp pain is not - Stop if something hurts sharply
  • Breathe normally - Don't hold your breath
  • Rest days are important - Muscles need recovery time
  • Consistency beats intensity - A little every day beats a lot once a week
