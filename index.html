💪 Exercise Programme

Functional Exercises – No Equipment Needed

Our 12-week progressive programme focuses on the movements that matter most: getting up from a chair, climbing stairs, walking confidently, and building the strength your new joint needs.

  • Personalised for hip or knee replacement
  • Functional movements – sit to stand, stairs, balance, floor transfers
  • Progressive – starts gentle, builds over 12 weeks
  • Video guidance for every exercise
  • Track your progress and build streaks
  • 20-30 minutes, 3-5 days per week

Why We Built Joint Journey

The UK has some of the longest waiting times for joint replacement surgery in the developed world. Hundreds of thousands of people spend months – sometimes over a year – in pain, losing fitness, gaining weight, and feeling anxious about their surgery.

We believe that waiting time doesn't have to be wasted time.

Joint Journey was created to turn that wait into an opportunity. An opportunity to strengthen the muscles around your joint, reach a healthier weight, understand what's coming, and build the positive mindset that research shows is the single greatest predictor of a good outcome.

This isn't a replacement for medical advice. It's a companion for the journey – from the moment you're listed for surgery to the day you walk into hospital, ready.

Every exercise you do, every healthy meal you make, every moment you spend preparing – it all counts. And it all adds up.
