1. About the Service

Joint Journey (jointjourney.org) is a free digital prehabilitation programme designed to help people preparing for hip or knee replacement surgery. It provides exercise programmes, nutritional guidance, mental wellbeing support, and practical preparation advice.

The service is operated from the United Kingdom and is primarily intended for UK-based users, though it is accessible worldwide.

4. Your Responsibilities
