## 1. Role
The Advisor is appointed to the Company's **Clinical Advisory Board** as the
**[physiotherapy / dietetics / psychology / patient]** advisor, on the terms of

## 2. Services / deliverables
The Advisor agrees to provide the following, within their professional competence:
- Review the **[physiotherapy / nutrition / mental-health]** content of the product
  and provide written sign-off (initial and on material change), recorded in the
  Company's content review and sign-off log.
- Contribute to identifying clinical hazards and mitigations in their domain.
- Attend approximately **[4] meetings per year** and provide reasonable async input.
- Estimated commitment: **approximately [2–4] hours per quarter.**

## 5. Independence & conflicts of interest
- The Advisor will declare all relevant interests on joining and keep them current
  in the Company's Conflict of Interest Register.
- The Advisor will disclose any connection to an NHS organisation that may
  commission or purchase the product, and will not participate in decisions where
  they have a material conflict.

## 6. Confidentiality
- The Advisor will keep confidential all non-public Company information and will
  not use or disclose it except to perform this role. This survives termination.

## 7. Intellectual property
- All content, materials, feedback and work product the Advisor creates **for the
  Company** under this agreement are assigned to the Company. The Advisor retains
  their own pre-existing IP and general professional knowledge.

## 8. Status & professional responsibility
- The Advisor is an **independent advisor**, not an employee, and does not provide
  clinical care to users through this role.
- The Advisor will act within their professional registration and scope, and will
  maintain their registration and (where applicable) professional indemnity.
- The Company (via its Clinical Safety Officer) holds responsibility for the
  product and its deployment.

## 9. Use of name
- The Company **may** name the Advisor and their title/role as a clinical advisor
  in materials and to trusts/funders, **only** with the Advisor's written consent
  to the specific wording, and only while this agreement is in force and the
  Advisor's sign-offs remain current.

## 10. Liability
- The Advisor provides advice in good faith. To the extent permitted by law, the
  Advisor is not liable for the Company's decisions or use of the advice beyond
  its scope. [Confirm wording with a solicitor.]

- This agreement is governed by the law of **England and Wales**.
