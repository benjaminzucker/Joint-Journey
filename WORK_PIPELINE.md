# Joint Journey — Work Pipeline

_Living document. Last updated: 25 June 2026_

## 🎯 North Star
Digital prehabilitation + recovery community for people awaiting surgery.
Wedge: hip & knee replacement. Platform: prehab for ANY major surgery.
Free until we charge (patient OR institution). No freemium split —
the whole product is free, then the whole product is paid.

---

## ✅ Status Snapshot (DONE / LIVE)
- Responsive web app (landing + full app) on Netlify
- Firebase Auth + Cloud Firestore (europe-west2)
- Four pillars: Exercises, Nutrition, Mindset, Getting Ready
- Personalised programme levels + dynamic programme length
- Oxford Hip/Knee Score in onboarding (baseline for outcomes)
- Exercise video links already in place (cartoons later, once funded)
- Adaptive dashboard (mood, streaks, surgery-prep nudges)
- Demo/surgeon-preview account
- Privacy, terms, disclaimer, GDPR consent, Google Analytics
